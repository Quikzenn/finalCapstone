Our mission

It is our passion and our mission to give all the incredibly talented musicians in this country - whether they be just starting out on their journey or years in the making - an open door to showcase to the world their gift. Many of us here at Acoustic Life are musicians and understand how difficult a life of performing can be. So we are here for you, to help open some doors and to help enrich the artistic scene with your talent.

Our objectives

  • To provide an open, friendly, online space for artists to create, communicate with each other and with venue owners or event managers.
  • To see the grassroots creative spaces of this country gain a greater voice
  • To try our very best to ensure that the great talent that works away day after day creating great art is not missed and never given its chance.
